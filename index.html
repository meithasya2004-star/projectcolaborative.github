The Impact Technology On Traditional Industries

Thesis

Technology has become an important part of our lives. It has changed many aspects of society, including traditional industries such as agriculture, manufacturing, and retail. While technology brings many benefits, it also causes challenges that must be faced by traditional workers and businesses.
